Welcome to Little Scholars

Service Before Self

The Little Scholars school is a co-educational CBSE affiliated institution, established in 1983 at Bhalla Farm in Kashipur, U.S. Nagar. At the school, we approach our curriculum from a philosophical and eclectic perspective, with the goal of developing each individual’s potential to the fullest.

The school aims to promote an institution that welcomes children without discrimination of caste, color, creed, or status in any respect whatsoever. Indian traditional values are instilled in our students, helping them grow with an awareness of our society and the roots of our culture. Each child is encouraged to cultivate strong ethical values and a moral fiber that will make them worthy members of the global community.
